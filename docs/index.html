Privacy Policy for MobileHero

Last Updated: January 29, 2026

1. Introduction

MobileHero ("we," "our," or "us") provides this Privacy Policy to inform users of our policies and procedures regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information. By using the MobileHero mobile application (the "App"), you consent to the practices described herein.

2. Data Collection and Usage

A. Personal Information

We do not collect, store, or process any Personal Identification Information (PII) on our own infrastructure. We do not require account registration or the submission of names, email addresses (except for support inquiries), or phone numbers.

B. Sensitive Permissions (Camera)

The App requests access to your device’s camera for the sole purpose of scanning QR codes to facilitate local connection to the MobileHero PC server.

  • Local Processing: Image data is processed in real-time on the device.
  • No Storage: No images or video frames are saved to the device gallery or transmitted to our servers.

C. Precise Location

This App does not collect or track your GPS location.

The App integrates third-party SDKs to provide essential functions. These third parties may collect information according to their own privacy policies:

Provider Purpose Data Collected Policy Link

RevenueCat Subscriptions Anonymized App User IDs, purchase history, transaction timestamps. RevenueCat Policy

4. Data Security and Retention

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect the minimal data generated during your session.

  • Retention: Since we do not host user data on our servers, we do not retain a database of personal information.
  • Third-Party Retention: Data collected by third-party SDKs (e.g., AdMob) is retained according to their specific retention schedules.

5. Data Deletion and User Rights

Under Google Play’s Data Safety mandates and global regulations (GDPR/CCPA):

  • Right to Delete: Because we do not store personal data or account information on our servers, there is no data for us to delete upon request.
  • Third-Party Data: Users may reset their Advertising ID or opt-out of personalized ads via their Android device settings (Settings > Google > Ads).
  • Inquiries: For any data-related requests, please contact the email provided in Section 8.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information via support channels, we will delete it immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify users of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
